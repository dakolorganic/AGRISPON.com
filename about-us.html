Who We Are

Rooted in humble beginnings, Dakol Organic began with a simple yet powerful belief that when nature and science come together, they open a path to sustainable and meaningful progress, enriching life for all.

Our story traces back to 2008 in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah, where our founder was inspired to give back to the local community through innovative biotechnology and sustainable solutions discovered during his time abroad. What began with the AGRISPON trademark has grown into an ongoing mission to introduce trusted, high-quality products that nurture environmental wellness and agricultural vitality.

At Dakol Organic, we are guided by a vision to inspire conscious living through a holistic approach to health and sustainability. We are dedicated to delivering reliable, forward-thinking solutions that help communities, farmers, and industries find harmony between productivity and environmental care.

Julius Masudal
Managing Director

Julius holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Finance and Marketing from the State University of New York at Buffalo. He served as a senior financial analyst at Ingram Micro, one of the world's leading technology distributors, where he gained extensive experience in mergers and acquisitions, budgeting, financial planning and analysis, and strategic planning for large American multinationals. He founded Dakol Organic as an initiative to champion the exclusive AGRISPON trademark and its role in advancing regenerative agricultural growth.

Datuk Rayner Stuel Galid
Technical Director

Datuk Rayner holds a Master of Marine Affairs (MMA) from the University of Rhode Island. He was a former Director of Fisheries with a distinguished career of 36 years in the Sabah Civil Service. In 2013, he was awarded the Panglima Gemilang Darjah Kinabalu (PGDK) by the Sabah state government, conferring upon him the title of Datuk. He has contributed to elevating the profile of the Sabah Civil Service by leading various national and international committees, including several under the BIMP-EAGA framework.
